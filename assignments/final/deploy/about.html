About Us

Jim Buckmaster

CEO

Jim Buckmaster is a computer programmer and has been the CEO of craigslist since 2000. In addition, he currently manages the craigslist Charitable Fund and is the executive producer for craigslist TV. After many years, he has been dedicated towards maintaining craigslist’s public service mission, non-corporate vibe, and staff of less than 50. He has worked on craigslist’s previous homepage design, community moderation system, discussion forums, serach engine, and many more functions prior to it’s redesign and now currently works in managment.

Craig Newmark

Founder

Craig Newmark is the original founder of craigslist and retired from his position in 2000 after giving the position of CEO to Jim Buckmaster. He originally created craigslist in 1996 as a place where people could freely and easily swap information and made it into a private for-profit company in 1999. In 2016 he created The Craig Newmark Foundation and has been an active philanthropist. He has been a philanthropist ever since 2004 and created the Craig Newmark Philanthropies as a result of this.
